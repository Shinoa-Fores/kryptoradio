Bitcoin Radio

Connect to the bitcoin network everywhere – even without the Internet!

General description

I am developing a project named bitcoin-broadcast. Its goal is to enable bitcoin transactions, block serialization and transmission over radio waves. Any one-way digital transmission path with sufficient error correction is suitable for this project. The overall goal is to use public broadcasting, e.g., digital television (DVB-T) or subcarriers of FM radio transmission. At early stages it is natural to use license-free frequencies.

The projects consists of two separate applications: transmitting serializer and receiving deserializer. The former connects to the the bitcoin network, receives transactions and blocks, then puts them into a queue, which is processed according to priorities. The result is sent to serial communication apparatus. On the other end, the receiver connects to the serial communication apparatus, processes the received transactions and blocks, and sends them to bitcoin nodes. The data stream can contain other information, such as exchange rates between bitcoins and traditional currencies.

This scheme makes it easy to construct affordable receivers that do not need mobile data connections in order to follow bitcoin traffic an to react to the received bitcoin payments. This would make it possible to build bitcoin counterpart for cash payment terminals, e.g., coin operated self-service laundry. If the receiver application followed only transactions relevant to itself, it could be build even with an 8-bit microcontroller. The bill of materials for this kind of receiver would be extremely low.

Already done:

  • Serializer connects and stays in the bitcoin network.
  • Serializer knows how to send transactions and blocks to serial port.
  • Serializer optimizes the sent blocks by referencing to already sent transactions, and does not retransmit them in a new block (exactly as the bitcoin network normally operates).

Not yet finished:

  • Proper transaction prioritization. Currently vulnerable to penny flooding.
  • Serial traffic format needs rethinking and someone else's opinion.
  • Serial traffic signing. The receiver needs to be sure that the data has not been forged.

Not yet started:

  • Receiver applicaltion (deserializer).
  • Testing in real radio network.
  • Sending exchange rates.

The application is programmed in C (gnu99) and it depends on Glib, LibSSL and Linux. It is licensed under GNU GPL v3. The sources are in GitHub: https://github.com/zouppen/bitcoin-broadcast. The site also tells how you can support the project financially. Even more useful would be if you know and want to help with the execution of the project!

Financing

I will now focus on distribution and financing. Personally, this is the more uninteresting part of the project. If someone is interested in these practical matters, please contact me.

The pilot stage could be executed during a couple of months at night time (distribution between midnight and 6 a.m.) and smaller distribution area, e.g. channel group E, until the system is stable enough to be used for 24 hours in the whole country. It would be possible to receive the pilot stage transmissions in most areas of Finland.

Because crowdfunding is difficult in Finland, the reward for financing the project could be an advertisement, which the startup would sell. The contributors would be mentioned in the web page but each contributor could create a message that would be randomly assigned to the radio transmitted data stream, weighted according to the amount of money contributed. Maybe something like this could work.

I have had a quick look at bitcoin crowdfunding. My first impression is that the available platforms are not very good either technologically or by the number of users. If someone has ideas how to collect seed funds for this project, please inform me. A minimum of 2000 euros (about 5 BTC) would be needed for the pilot stage even if I do not count my own work. More would be needed if the tests are promising and a wider distribution is in order.

(If the project's bitcoin address would mysteriously receive >5 BTC during the next few days, it would be a lot easier because financial planning could be pushed forward and the pilot stage could be started early.)

-- Zouppen
