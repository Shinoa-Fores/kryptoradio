Kryptoradio transmitters in Finland

Kryptoradio uses Digita's multiplex E. All transmitters have horizontal polarization. Coordinates are in EUREF-FIN format which is virtually equal to WGS84. Coordinates have an accuracy of 1/60 degrees so you may expect an error of about 1 km in longitude and 0.5 km in latitude (on 60°). Data source: Ficora. See also Digita's map view.
